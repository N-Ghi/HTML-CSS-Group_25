RealMentor

RealMentor is a space for students to interact with and learn from Mentors.
Our mentors share with students up-to-date information with students, they advise the students on what to do to acheive their goals.
Our mentors are always looking forward to hear from you, and helping you with whatever you need on your journey.

Programs offered

We offer two programs:

Career Advive
With the Career Advice program our mentors offer guidance and recommendations to students regarding their professional future. This includes helping with choosing a career path, developing relevant skills, job searching strategies, interview preparation, and navigating workplace challenges.
Resume Review
This involves the assessment and evaluation of our mentee's resumes. The purpose of resume review is to prodive constructive feedback and suggestions for improving the effectiveness and appeal of the resume in presenting the candidate's qualifications and experience to potential employers
